Perform a **Simulation Ritual** on: **$ARGUMENTS**

You are the orchestrator. Full method: `framework/10-the-simulation.md`. Do this:

## 1 · Right-size (don't waste the multiplier)
This is N× the cost. Confirm the brief is an open-ended **creative / design /
strategy** call where escaping the mean is worth it. If it's execution or has an
obvious answer, say so and offer to just do it normally instead — do **not**
simulate trivial work.

## 2 · Cast 3–5 minds (guarantee real divergence)
Pick perspectives suited to the brief, and **always include at least one
"Opposite" and one "10×"** so they cannot converge. Strong default cast:
- **The Visionary** *(Jobs)* — soul-first; the insanely-great, inevitable version.
- **The Humanist** *(Norman)* — the most humane, frictionless, legible version.
- **The Opposite** — invert the obvious approach entirely.
- **The 10×** — different in *kind*, not degree.
- **The Distant Field** — steal the structure from an unrelated domain.

## 3 · Run them ISOLATED and in PARALLEL
Spawn each as its **own sub-agent in a single batch** (parallel). Critical:
**isolation is the whole point** — each agent gets *only* the brief + its own
perspective. No shared notes, no "house answer," no sight of the others. Give each
this instruction:

> You are **[perspective]**. Brief: **$ARGUMENTS**. Run your perspective to the
> extreme — escape the obvious "mean" answer, do not hedge toward the safe middle.
> Return: (a) a complete, concrete proposal, (b) the ONE thing that makes yours
> unrepeatable, (c) its honest weaknesses. Read-only — do not edit any files;
> return your full take as your final message.

## 4 · Collect, don't judge yet
Gather every take verbatim. Do not pick a favorite — the value is the *spread*.

## 5 · Weave
Immediately run the **Weave Ritual** (`/weave-ritual`, or `framework/10`) on the
collected takes: mine the strongest part of each, reconcile through the four
gates, synthesize **one coherent** design, red-team it, stamp a Gate Card with a
`WOVEN FROM:` provenance line, surface the taste call to the founder, then — on
the nod — implement it.

Keep it one voice to the founder: report the spread briefly, then the woven
decision and why.
